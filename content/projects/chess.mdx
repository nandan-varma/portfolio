Chess Online is developed to play chess online with flexiblity to upload and upgrade my own chess engine which is in active development

## Features

- Play with friends
- Play with AI
- Play on board
- User specific metrics(under development)
- login system for future use.


## Built with

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org)
- [tailwindcss](https://tailwindcss.com)
- Deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com)

To learn more about my Chess Engine [Meadows](/projects/meadows).
