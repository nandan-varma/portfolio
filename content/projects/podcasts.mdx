Podcasts is developed for submission for a hackathon in 5 days

## Features

- Store audio in database
- Play and download audio files
- Player controls for online playing
- login system for favorites and reccomendations

## What I learnt

- Communications and Work allotment
- MongoDB

## Built with

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org)
- [tailwindcss](https://tailwindcss.com)
- Deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com)
- Database on [mongodb](https://www.mongodb.com)
- Authentication on [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com)
