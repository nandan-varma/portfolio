## Puzzles tests your ability in

- [Analytical test](https://puzzle.nandanvarma.com/analytical) where you find words from table.
- [Memory test](https://puzzle.nandanvarma.com/memory) where you find similar cards.
- [Speed test](https://puzzle.nandanvarma.com/speed) where you play whack-a-mole.
- Many more to come if i have time.

## What I learnt

- Code Modularity
- Creativity
- Understanding requirements

## Built with

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org)
- [tailwindcss](https://tailwindcss.com)
- Deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com)
- Database and Authentication on [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com)
